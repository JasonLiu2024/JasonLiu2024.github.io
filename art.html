Highly Customizable Circular Maze

Background: Inspired by graph search algorithms and the lack of customizability in existing solutions, I developed a tool to quickly generate circular mazes in SVG format. In comparison, existing solution either have a much lower degree of customizability or approximate circles with poly-lines. Both are important limitations to artists who are interested in creating 3D models based on mazes

This tool is easily customizable in terms of:

  1. Number of rings
  2. Radius of each ring
  3. Density of the paths
  4. Any particular path you would like to keep in the generated model
