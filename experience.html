Presentation at the ASU-GSV Summit 2023

My team (Legal Linguists) and I prototyped and demo-ed Legal Lingo, a translation tool tailored to legal documents
We built this tool to advance social and racial equality and welfare.

  • As a group of immigrants and international students, we share the experience of translating important legal documents to our parents, who aren't as familiar with English as us
  • Legal Lingo carries our hope to bring more opportunities and resources to those on the other side of the language barrier

    • We pitched our idea to an audience of more than 60 entrepreneurs, educators, and philanthropists
    Legal Lingo won the 2022 Create-a-thon by MIT RAISE and SureStart
